My name is Prince Zizhuang Wang (my Chinese name Wang Zizhuang means a mighty prince in English). I am a 3rd year Computer Science major at [College of Creative Studies](https://ccs.ucsb.edu/), [University of California Santa Barbara](https://www.ucsb.edu/), advised by Prof. [William Wang](https://sites.cs.ucsb.edu/~william/) and Prof. [Yu-Xiang Wang](https://sites.cs.ucsb.edu/~yuxiangw/). I am in our UCSB BS/MS program and I am expecting to get my Bachelor Science and Master degree next year. My research interests include: Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Natural Language Processing, Generative Models, and Variational Inference.
